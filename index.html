The 0% fee, instant*

The mining reward is transfered to your wallet instantly whenever a block gets mined.

However, as part of the Garlicoin protocol, you will not be able to use these new coins until the network as a whole has mined another 100 blocks.

Depending on the wallet you are using, these coins might or might not show up on your balance in the mean time. If in doubt, please use a block explorer to verify you were properly payed out.
